About Fateh Travels

Fateh Travels is a trusted intercity transport service based in Mohali and Chandigarh. We specialize in providing safe, comfortable, and timely rides to airports and major cities like Ludhiana, Patiala, Amritsar, Nangal, and Una.

With our 2025 Dzire Tour CNG vehicles, we ensure a smooth journey with experienced drivers and excellent customer service. Our goal is to make travel easy, reliable, and affordable for everyone.

Whether you need a quick airport transfer or a customized tour, Fateh Travels is your partner on the road.
